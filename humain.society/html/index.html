HOW YOU CAN HELP
Many ways to help
Make a memorial donation

Honor the life of a special friend, pet or other loved one with a memorial gift to the Humane Society. With your memorial gift, you’re helping the HSUS fight for all animals. This special tribute honors your loved one while helping to create a more humane society for animals and people alike.

Make a gift donation

Honor a friend, relative or even a pet with a gift donation to the Humane Society. Gift donations are a great alternative to a traditional birthday, anniversary or holiday gift, you can even send one just to say you care!.

Grants from Donor Advised Funds

Are you looking for additional ways to help animals? You can get involved with animal issues in your own neck of the woods. Let us know the actions you’re willing to take and the issues that interest you most by filling out a short form, and we will connect with you on ways you can get engaged.
