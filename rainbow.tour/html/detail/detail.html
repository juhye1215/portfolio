ATV TOURS AT KUALOA


Take an outdoor adventure on one of O’ahu’s best ATV tours. Kualoa’s one and two-hour ATV rides take guests through scenic valleys and remote areas rarely visited by others. Our guided ATV tours go out rain or shine – crossing streams and diggin’ through dirt are just part of the fun!
TIP: Advanced reservations are recommended as many tours sell out 2-3 weeks in advance.

Kualoa prides itself on providing the best guest experience, so our “guide-to-guest” ratios are very low, with one guide to every six or ten guests in most cases (except for the Hollywood Movie Sites Tour). This contributes to very limited availability, so please accept our apologies if the tour you want is not available. You can also search for single seats as they will usually be available on tours at the last minute if you are willing to split up.

RESTRICTIONS


1. Minimum age is 16 years old
2. Covered shoes required
3. Must attend briefing
4. Signed Liability Waiver required
5. Long pants & sunscreen highly encouraged

Q. What is the cancellation policy? A. Please call 808-237-7321 at least 24 hours notice before the tour, or scheduled pick up time, to avoid a full charge penalty.
Q. If it rains will the tours still go out? A. The safety of our guests is our number priority and any adverse weather conditions are taken into consideration. Generally, unless there are high winds or lightning threatening (which happens very infrequently) our tours go out rain or shine. Kualoa Ranch reserves the right to cancel a tour or reservation if conditions are deemed unsafe. If we do so, we will refund your money for the portion that may have been cancelled. Some tours, such as the ATV tours, are actually preferred by many guests in rainy weather and when it rains you’ ll probably be able to see many beautiful waterfalls in our iconic mountain peaks. If you are on island and have any questions about weather or anything else, you can always call into Kualoa Reservations at 808-237-7321 from 7am til 5:30pm daily.
Q. Are there any tours without guides? A. All of our activities are guided tours. Touring our 4000 acre Private Nature Reserve is our passion! We strive to teach a little Hawaiian History, Paniolo (cowboy) history and Kualoa Movie History in each tour and your guide will provide this experience.
Q. Is packing/doubling allowed on Horseback or ATVs? A. Packing/doubling are not allowed on these tours. Our equipment accommodates one person per Horse/ATV. Our new Multi Passenger ATV Raptors can accommodate children 5 years and older with adults.
